Understanding the Product Reselling Partner Model A product reselling partner is an intermediary who sells your company’s products or services for you and under an agreed upon arrangement. This partnership model is ideal for businesses to reach into new markets and territories at a fraction of the cost of the sales overhead associated with direct sell into markets. Resellers can be a value-added partner who provides a complementary service or can be just a standalone even on a contractual basis to the distributor for the purpose of product placement. The Power of Retention in Reselling Partnerships It is considerably less expensive to retain an existing customer than it is to acquire a new customer. Research has shown that a 5% customer retention can increase profits by 25% to 95%. Reselling partners play an important role in retaining customers because of their delivery of ongoing support, tailored recommendations, and special loyalty programs specific to the needs of existing customers. Retention strategies can include: Customer Loyalty Programs: Rewarding customers for making repeat purchases through discounts and specials. Personalized Communication : Using CRM tools to stay in touch with customer activity in order to connect on their needs and wants. Support First: Promptly answering customer inquiries and requests establishes thickness and trust with your customer.
