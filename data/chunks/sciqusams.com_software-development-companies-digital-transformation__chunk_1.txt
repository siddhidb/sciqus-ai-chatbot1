Industry Trends Driving Software Development Growth 1. Growth of artificial intelligence and machine learning Artificial intelligence (AI) is causing a disruption in software development by enabling intelligent automation, predictive analytics, and better decision-making. By 2025, AI software development on a global scale is expected to generate a new $126 billion market growing existing trends towards greater adoption and use of software development across all industries. 2. Widespread adoption of cloud-based computing Cloud-based solutions for software development are changing the way businesses operate; reducing infrastructure ownership and enhancing collaboration. Recently, more than 94% of organizations reported that they are adopting cloud services further confirming expanding demand for software development. 3. Blockchain Technology Integrated- Software development and blockchain technology is often being incorporated into or integrated with software solutions for security in transactions, data integrity and to facilitate decentralized applications. Experts have suggested the blockchain market will expand at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 56.3% during the period from 2022 to 2029.
