Real-World Example: Apple’s Authorized Reseller ModelApple has developed a powerful product reselling partnerships strategy of a network of authorized resellers. Apple trusts that these partners will extend the customer experience, deepen Apple’s reach into the market with localized customer service, build a relationship to upsell (AppleCare) or recommend accessories (AirPods and cases). Together, with authorized resellers partnership, Apple’s effort to delight extensions to Apple’s retention rate have contributed to its an impressive 90% loyalty retention rate with baseline number of users, iPhone users.
