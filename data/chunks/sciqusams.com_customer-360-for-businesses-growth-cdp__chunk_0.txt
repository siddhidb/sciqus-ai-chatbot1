Customer 360 is a method of combining all your customer data—from sales and marketing to support and billing—into one single, real-time profile. This profile includes behavior, purchases, communication history, and more. Unlike traditional CRMs, which often keep contact details static, a Customer 360 platform like SciqusAMS acts as a Composable CDP. It integrates with your tools, powers analytics, and activates smart actions automatically. Why IT Leaders Need Customer 360 Today’s customers want fast, personalized service.
