What is a Quarterly Business Review (QBR)? A QBR is a scheduled meeting between a business and its clients to evaluate past performance, discuss key challenges, and plan for the future. Unlike routine check-ins, QBRs take a strategic approach, focusing on long-term goals, key performance indicators (KPIs), and ways to optimize collaboration for mutual growth.
