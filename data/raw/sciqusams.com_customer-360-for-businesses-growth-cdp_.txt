What is Customer 360?
Customer 360 is a method of combining all your customer data—from sales and marketing to support and billing—into one single, real-time profile. This profile includes behavior, purchases, communication history, and more.
Unlike traditional CRMs, which often keep contact details static, a Customer 360 platform like SciqusAMS acts as a Composable CDP. It integrates with your tools, powers analytics, and activates smart actions automatically.
Why IT Leaders Need Customer 360
Today’s customers want fast, personalized service. That requires more than scattered data.
Customer 360 helps you:
Spot churn risks early
Suggest upsells using behavioral data
Align teams from sales to support
Connect with your ERP or data warehouse to make better decisions
Why CRMs Alone Are No Longer Enough
 
Many CRMs face three big issues:
Siloed Data:
 Sales teams see one view, support teams another. There’s no single source of truth.
Inactive Data:
 The CRM stores info, but doesn’t trigger smart actions.
Duplicate Records:
 Without identity resolution, clients get mismanaged.
Customer 360 solves these issues by syncing everything in real time, helping you make decisions faster and smarter.
Composable CDPs: Flexible and Scalable
A composable CDP doesn’t require a full tech overhaul. You keep your current CRM, support system, and marketing tools. SciqusAMS connects them together.
It combines data from emails, support tickets, product usage, campaign metrics, and more into one accurate profile. No more duplicates. No missed opportunities.
