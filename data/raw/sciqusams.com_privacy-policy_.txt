Introduction
Sciqus AMS is committed to upholding the highest standards of data privacy and security. This Privacy Policy comprehensively delineates the methods by which we collect, process, store, and safeguard your personal information when you engage with our platform. By accessing and utilizing Sciqus AMS, you consent to the data practices detailed herein.
1. Information We Collect
Sciqus AMS systematically collects and processes data to optimize user experience, ensure regulatory compliance, and enhance service efficiency. The categories of data we collect include:
1.1 Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
Full name, email address, and contact number
Business affiliations and job titles (where applicable)
Billing details and transactional data (processed through secure third-party gateways)
1.2 System-Generated and Behavioral Data
Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, device specifications, browser metadata, and operating system details
User engagement metrics, including session duration, page views, and navigation behavior
Tracking technologies, including cookies, web beacons, and log files (see 
Cookies and Tracking Mechanisms
 below)
1.3 Third-Party and Publicly Sourced Data
Data obtained from external databases, social media interactions, and business partners
2. Utilization of Collected Data
Sciqus AMS employs user data for the following purposes:
To facilitate service delivery and ensure seamless platform functionality
To execute transactions and issue billing statements
To maintain system integrity and detect security threats
To enhance customer engagement through personalized content, where user consent is obtained
To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
3. Legal Framework for Data Processing
The lawful basis upon which we process personal information includes:
Contractual Necessity
 – Enabling service provision in accordance with user agreements
Legitimate Interests
 – Enhancing operational efficiency and user experience
Legal Compliance
 – Adhering to statutory and regulatory mandates
Explicit Consent
 – Acquiring affirmative user authorization for discretionary data processing activities
4. Data Sharing and Disclosures
Sciqus AMS does not engage in the sale or rental of personal information. However, data sharing may occur under the following circumstances:
Service Providers and Vendors
 – Collaboration with third-party entities responsible for payment processing, data hosting, analytics, and customer support
Legal and Regulatory Authorities
 – Disclosure as mandated by judicial orders or statutory obligations
Corporate Transactions
 – Transfer of data in the event of business mergers, acquisitions, or restructuring
5. Cross-Border Data Transfers
User data may be transmitted and stored in jurisdictions outside the user’s country of residence. Sciqus AMS ensures that such transfers adhere to stringent data protection frameworks, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and other regulatory safeguards.
6. Security Framework and Risk Mitigation
We implement robust security measures to mitigate risks associated with data breaches, including:
End-to-end encryption protocols, including Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology
Periodic vulnerability assessments and penetration testing
Multi-tiered access control mechanisms
While Sciqus AMS endeavors to uphold optimal data security standards, users are encouraged to exercise precautionary measures, such as utilizing strong authentication credentials.
7. Data Retention Policy
Personal information is retained only for the duration necessary to fulfill its intended purpose, including:
Service provision and contractual fulfillment
Compliance with statutory retention requirements
Resolution of disputes and enforcement of company policies Once data is no longer required, it is systematically anonymized or securely deleted.
8. User Rights and Control Mechanisms
Subject to jurisdiction-specific data protection regulations, users retain the following rights:
Access
 – Request disclosure of stored personal data
Rectification
 – Amend inaccuracies in personal records
Erasure
 – Request deletion of personal data (subject to legal exemptions)
Opt-Out
 – Withdraw consent for marketing communications
Data Portability
 – Transfer personal data to an alternative service provider
To exercise these rights, users may contact Sciqus AMS at [Insert Contact Email].
9. Cookies and Tracking Mechanisms
Sciqus AMS employs tracking technologies to:
Enhance site performance and usability
Personalize content and user experience
Analyze behavioral data for service optimization
Users may adjust browser settings to manage cookie preferences. However, disabling cookies may limit certain functionalities.
10. External Links and Third-Party Platforms
Our platform may include links to external websites. Sciqus AMS is not liable for the data protection practices of such third parties, and users are encouraged to review their respective privacy policies.
11. Data Protection for Minors
Sciqus AMS services are not intended for individuals below the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If inadvertent data collection occurs, users may request deletion by contacting us.
12. Amendments to the Privacy Policy
Sciqus AMS reserves the right to revise this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in legal, technological, or business frameworks. Significant updates will be communicated through official channels, and the most recent version will be accessible on our website.
