FAQ's
General Product Capability & Differentiation
 
 
 
 
 
How does your software support scalable account management as our team grows?
Sciqus AMS is designed with 
modular scalability
, allowing you to onboard new users, assign roles, and manage complex account hierarchies with ease. It adapts to growing teams with granular access control and collaborative workflows.
 
 
 
 
 
What makes your proposal generation feature more efficient than manual methods?
Our proposal generator pulls live data from connected CRMs, pricing sheets, and templates, enabling 
automated, data-driven proposals
 in minutes—eliminating manual errors and formatting inconsistencies.
 
 
 
 
 
How does your platform ensure brand consistency across proposals and customer communication?
We offer 
centralized brand templates
, pre-approved content blocks, and visual consistency tools that lock fonts, logos, and color schemes—ensuring brand integrity across all documents
 
 
 
 
 
Can your solution handle complex B2B account structures and multiple stakeholders?
Yes, Sciqus AMS supports 
multi-tiered B2B account management
 with stakeholder tagging, relationship mapping, and multi-contact engagement history.
 
 
 
 
 
How does your software support Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs)?
The platform includes 
QBR-ready templates
, customer health summaries, renewal forecasts, and performance dashboards—automatically populated and exportable for strategic review meetings.
 
 Customer Data & Insights
 
 
 
 
 
 
Is customer data centralized and accessible across departments in real-time?
 
 
 
 
Yes, Sciqus AMS provides a 
single source of truth
 with real-time customer data synchronization across departments, enhancing alignment and decision-making.
 
 
 
 
 
How does your platform assist with customer journey mapping and tracking touchpoints?
 
 
 
 
Our journey visualization tool tracks 
key milestones, communications, renewals, escalations, and upsells
—enabling full visibility into customer lifecycle stages.
 
 
 
 
 
Does the system provide churn risk detection or predictive analytics?
 
 
 
 
Yes, built-in 
predictive analytics and churn detection models
 flag at-risk accounts using activity patterns, sentiment, engagement frequency, and KPI deviations.
 
 
 
 
 
Can we track customer health scores and long-term engagement?
 
 
 
 
Absolutely. Customer health is scored using 
custom and system metrics
, engagement data, NPS (Net Promoter Score), and touchpoint frequency to visualize account trends.
 
 
 
 
 
What kind of dashboards and reporting tools are available for customer insights?
 
 
 
 
We offer 
real-time, customizable dashboards
 with drill-down reporting for renewals, pipeline, engagement, sentiment, and churn—available by user role or department.
Automation & Efficiency
 
 
 
 
 
Can tasks and workflows be automated based on customer lifecycle events?
Yes, workflows can be 
triggered by lifecycle milestones
 (e.g., onboarding complete, renewal due) to auto-assign tasks, emails, or alerts.
 
 
 
 
 
Does the tool auto-generate proposals using the latest business data and pricing models?
Yes, the platform syncs with your 
live pricing database and business rules
, enabling auto-populated, up-to-date proposal documents.
 
 
 
 
 
How does your system streamline renewals and upsell opportunities?
Automated alerts and 
renewal scorecards
 are sent to managers ahead of time, while AI models identify 
upsell potential
 based on usage and engagement data.
 
 
 
 
 
Is there a feature for automating reminders for follow-ups and QBRs?
Yes, 
task automation and reminder scheduling
 ensure teams never miss critical follow-ups, renewal checkpoints, or QBR planning.
 
 
 
 
 
How does your system accelerate the internal review process for client proposals?
Proposals follow a 
review-routing workflow
 with comment threads, version control, and e-approvals—reducing internal review time drastically.
Collaboration & Communication
 
 
 
 
 
Can multiple team members collaborate on a single account with role-based access?
Yes, Sciqus AMS enables 
multi-user collaboration
 with permission-based views, shared task boards, and team-specific access controls.
 
 
 
 
 
How does your software support real-time communication with customers and internal teams?
We support 
real-time in-app messaging
, email integrations, and Slack-like internal notes for seamless communication.
 
 
 
 
 
Is there a shared knowledge hub or documentation center?
Yes, a centralized 
knowledge repository
 allows teams to upload, tag, and share documents, FAQs, and playbooks by account or segment.
 
 
 
 
 
Do you offer internal notes or comment threads for account-level collaboration?
Each account includes a 
commenting system
 for teams to leave notes, feedback, or internal handovers visible only to team members.
 
 
 
 
 
Does your system offer in-app notifications or alerts for account updates?
Yes, 
customizable in-app alerts, emails, and mobile push notifications
 ensure users are notified of account changes, approvals, or escalations.
